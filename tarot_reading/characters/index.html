Character Introductions

Mansikka

Mansikka is a timeless dessert witch embodying the essence of the fairy tale world. With her whimsical demeanor and boundless knowledge, she traverses realms, spreading warmth and magic. Physically untouched by human sorrow, she seeks to defy the inevitable fate of her existence, balancing joy and melancholy as she guides others through their journeys.

Elena

Elena is the last of the deer-elves, living in the serene Border Forest of Zela. With her gentle spirit and deep connection to nature, she crafts powerful arrows and protects the magical creatures of her homeland. Though burdened by the loss of her parents, she holds on to their memories as she embarks on a quest for understanding, embodying innocence and resilience.

Filia Fair

Filia Fair, known as the "White Demon," is a mysterious reincarnation of a lamb that faced a tragic fate. Existing in a shadowy library, she wields a unique power to manipulate metal into a deadly weapon. Cold and seemingly emotionless, she is caught in a web of secrets and espionage, navigating the delicate balance between her dark past and the uncertainty of her future.
